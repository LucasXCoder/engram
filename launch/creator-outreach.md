# Creator outreach

The highest-leverage growth move: get a creator to share that someone built an AI of their channel.
One creator with a real audience sharing this = thousands of users instantly. Keep it short,
flattering, and low-ask. Lead with the live link — let the graph do the selling.

## Start here: Delta Trend Trading
You already built his brain. He's the obvious first ask.

> Hey Thomas — I'm a big fan and I built something with your channel I think you'll find cool.
>
> It's an open-source tool I made (Engram) that reads a creator's whole channel and turns it into
> an interactive "brain" — a graph of every idea, connected, that you can ask questions and get
> answers from *in your own frameworks*, each cited back to the exact video.
>
> I ran it on your channel as the flagship example. Here's the live graph: [link]
>
> No ask — just thought you'd get a kick out of seeing your ideas mapped out. Happy to take it
> down or tweak anything. If you think your audience would like it, even better.

## Generic template (any creator)

> Hi [name] — I built an open-source tool that turns a creator's YouTube channel into an
> interactive, queryable knowledge graph, and I used yours as a demo because [specific genuine
> reason].
>
> You can click around every idea in your channel and it links back to the source video: [link]
>
> Totally happy to adjust or remove it. If you like it, a share would make my week — but no
> pressure at all.

## Tips
- **Personalize the bracket** — reference a specific video/idea. Generic = ignored.
- **Lead with the live link**, not a pitch. The visual converts.
- **Make the ask tiny** ("thought you'd enjoy it") — a share is their idea, not your demand.
- **Pick creators 10k–500k subs** — big enough to matter, small enough to read DMs.
- Channels: X DM, YouTube "About" email, Discord, or community tab.
- **Offer to take it down** up front — removes the threat, builds trust.
